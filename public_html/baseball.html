The local baseball teams, the All Stars and the Snakes, have recently agreed to host several pitching, hitting, and fielding clinics for the Little League teams from State and Boone County next month. The teams that are coming include the Storms, the Badgers, the Tornado and the Rams. The Little League teams are holding a fundraiser tournament next Saturday in order to raise the funds need to get the teams to the All-Stars or Snakes training fields during the month.
